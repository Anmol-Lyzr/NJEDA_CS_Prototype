## Direct Loans — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/directloans/

### Summary
Direct NJEDA loans for NJ businesses committed to job creation/retention when conventional financing is not available.

### Amount (page headline)
- Up to $2M for fixed assets
- Up to $750k for working capital

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- Job creation/retention commitment (see page)
- DSCR requirement (business vs nonprofit)
- In operation at least 2 years
- Collateral required; home-based businesses ineligible

### Apply
- Use the NJEDA portal linked on the program page
