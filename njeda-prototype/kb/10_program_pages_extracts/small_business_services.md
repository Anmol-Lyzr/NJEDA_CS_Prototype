## Small Business Services — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/smallbusinessservices/

### Summary
Hub page listing many NJEDA small-to-mid-sized business programs and financing options, plus partner resources (CDFIs, technical assistance partners, and procurement technical assistance centers).

### Programs linked on the page (examples)
- Premier Lender Program
- NJ LEND
- Direct Loans
- Bond Financing
- Small Business Improvement Grant
- Small Business Lease Grant
- Small Business E-Commerce Support Program
- Small Business Bonding Readiness Assistance Program
