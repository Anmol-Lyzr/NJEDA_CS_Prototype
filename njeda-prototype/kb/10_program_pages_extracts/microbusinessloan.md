## Main Street Micro Business Loan — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/microbusinessloan/

### Summary
Financing up to $50,000 for eligible NJ micro businesses (≤10 full-time employees; ≤$1.5M annual revenues). The program page notes funding is fully subscribed and NJEDA is no longer accepting new applications.

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- ≤10 full-time employees
- NJ business location; formed at least 6 months prior to application
- Use of funds includes working capital, inventory, and limited equipment purchases
