## Emerge Program — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/emerge/

### Summary
The Emerge Program (Economic Recovery Act of 2020) provides **per-job tax credits** to projects that invest private capital in NJ and create or retain good-paying jobs. Statute extended program to **March 1, 2029**.

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- Create at least **35 new full-time jobs** (may be lower in some cases, e.g., targeted industries or “small business” per rules)
- Be located in an **eligible incentive location**
- Meet **minimum capital investment** requirements
- Meet required **net positive benefit** threshold
- Employee work-time and withholding requirements (see program page)

### Award sizing (high-level)
- Base credits per job vary by location/project characteristics
- Bonuses may apply; program page includes an award size calculator link
