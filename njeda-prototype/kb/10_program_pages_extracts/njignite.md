## NJ Ignite — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/njignite/

### Summary
Provides rent support grants for early-stage technology and life sciences startups locating in NJEDA-approved collaborative workspaces. The program page states the program is now closed.

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- Startup: early-stage technology or life science company (see qualification checklist on page)
- Workspace: must be an approved collaborative workspace (separate qualification process)
