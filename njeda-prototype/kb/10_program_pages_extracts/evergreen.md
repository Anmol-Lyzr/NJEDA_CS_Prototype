## NJ Innovation Evergreen Fund — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/evergreen

### Summary
Evergreen is a platform that raises capital via a corporate tax credit auction and partners with private venture capital firms to **co-invest** in qualified NJ-based early-stage companies.

### How it works (high-level)
- NJ sells state corporate tax credits in a competitive auction
- Proceeds support co-investment with approved venture firms

### What to do if you’re a startup
- Review the participating venture firms for fit
- Email non-confidential materials per the program page guidance
