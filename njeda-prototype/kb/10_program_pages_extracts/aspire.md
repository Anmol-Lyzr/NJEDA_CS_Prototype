## Aspire Program — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/aspire/

### Summary
Aspire is a **gap financing** tool supporting commercial, mixed-use, and residential real estate development projects, providing tax credits when a project is not economically feasible without the incentive.

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- Demonstrate a **financing gap** and/or below-market return without the incentive
- Located in a designated **Incentive Area**
- Developer equity participation of at least **20%**
- Net positive benefit to the State
- Residential projects must meet cost thresholds (see program page)

### Application
- Apply via program portal (see “Apply Here” on the page)
- Consultation with program staff is advised; fees are non-refundable
