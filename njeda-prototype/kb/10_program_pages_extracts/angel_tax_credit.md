## Angel Investor Tax Credit Program — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/angeltaxcredit/

### Summary
Provides a refundable tax credit for investors making qualified investments in **NJ emerging technology businesses**.

### Amount (page headline)
- 35% of qualified investment (potentially 40% with bonuses such as M/WBE or certain geographies)

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- Technology business: fewer than 150 employees; at least 75% work in NJ (per page headline)
- Company must conduct eligible activities in NJ (e.g., qualified research, pilot manufacturing, or commercialization of eligible technologies)
- Investor application must be submitted within **6 months** of investment (dual investor + company sections)

### How to apply
- Submit via the online application link on the program page
