## Brownfields Redevelopment Incentive Program (BRIP) — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/brownfield-redevelopment-incentive/

### Summary
Project-based, transferable tax credit to incentivize remediation, abatement, and demolition activities enabling redevelopment of brownfield sites. Page notes rules are being updated and the program is not currently accepting applications.

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- Covers specific remediation-related eligible costs (see program page)
- Applicant must not be the discharger/party responsible for contamination
- Prevailing wage requirements apply
- Some projects must demonstrate a financing gap (see page)
