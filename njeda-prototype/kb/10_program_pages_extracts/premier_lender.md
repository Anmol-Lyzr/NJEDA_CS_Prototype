## Premier Lender Program — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/premierlender/

### Summary
Partnership between NJEDA and participating banks providing loan participation/guarantee options and line of credit guarantees to support NJ businesses and nonprofits.

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- In operation at least 2 years
- DSCR requirement (varies by business vs nonprofit)
- Collateral required (fixed assets)
- Job creation/retention commitment (see page)
