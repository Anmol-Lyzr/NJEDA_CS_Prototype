## NJ LEND — NJEDA

Source: https://www.njeda.gov/njlend/

### Summary
3-year pilot program expanding NJEDA lending capacity. Eligible businesses can access financing up to $5M for owner-occupied commercial real estate, equipment, or working capital, either through direct NJEDA loans or via enrolled Premier Lenders.

### Key eligibility (high-level)
- NJ-based business
- For-profit operating ≥2 years (nonprofit ≥3 years)
- ≤750 employees
- Job creation/retention commitment (see page)
- Home-based businesses ineligible
